Maintaining your sweepa

Simply wash and rinse in soapy water when soiled. The bristles will not wear, tear or fall out. It is important that the broom head is correctly installed. Please ensure that the rubber broom head is pushed down all the way over the handle. The broom head should be covering the broom handle and the plastic joiners. No joins or plastic fittings will be visible when the broom head is correctly in place.
